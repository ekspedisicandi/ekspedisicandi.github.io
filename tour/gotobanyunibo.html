06:00 - 6:30 Journey of the Ratu Boko temple to Banyunibo (View Direction)


Under Boko hill between rice fields and a small grove of trees are called Banyunibo temple made by ancestors to worship the goddess of fertility and as an expression of gratitude. Reliefs on the walls of the temple depicts the goddess of fertility, surrounded kids
